Chef Dennis

Corporate Executive Chef

Chef Dennis joined the Big City Diner family in 2001, bringing his passion for quality food and culinary excellence to one of Hawaii's most beloved local restaurants. In 2002, he opened the Kailua location, helping establish it as a favorite gathering place for the Windward community.

From intimate gatherings to large-scale events, Chef Dennis works to ensure that every dish reflects the quality, consistency, and flavors that have become synonymous with Big City Diner.
